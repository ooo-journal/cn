Author Guide

Submission guidelines for extraordinary research

Submission Requirements

Formatting

No formatting required. We accept .docx, .txt, napkin sketches (scanned), and telepathic bursts (in beta). Our neural network will automatically format your submission to meet our standards.

Length

Articles can be any length, from a single sentence to a multi-volume epic. Our only requirement is that the content is sufficiently odd and thought-provoking.

Figures & Tables

Include any visual elements that enhance your research. We accept photographs, diagrams, abstract art, and even interpretive dance videos (uploaded as supplementary material).

References

Cite any sources that inspired your work. We accept traditional academic references, personal anecdotes, and citations of fictional works (with proper attribution).

Submission Process

1

Prepare Your Manuscript

Create your manuscript in any format. Remember, the weirder, the better. Our editorial team specializes in identifying the extraordinary in the mundane.

2

Submit Through the Portal

Upload your manuscript through our Submission Portal. You'll receive an immediate confirmation that your submission has entered our neural review queue.

3

Neural Review Process

Our AI system will first assess your submission's oddity quotient. If it passes, it will be assigned to human reviewers with expertise in your area of oddity.

4

Decision & Revision

You'll receive a decision within 24-48 hours. If revisions are requested, you'll have 7 days to resubmit. Accepted manuscripts will be published within 3-5 business days.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of research do you accept?

We accept any research that challenges conventional thinking and explores the extraordinary. From speculative science to philosophical musings, if it's out of the ordinary, we want to see it.

Do I need to have a academic background?

No formal academic background is required. We value creativity and originality above credentials. Whether you're a professional researcher or an amateur enthusiast, your ideas are welcome.

Is there a submission fee?

No, we do not charge submission fees. We believe that extraordinary ideas should be accessible to everyone, regardless of financial resources.

How can I check the status of my submission?

You can track your submission's progress through our Author Dashboard. You'll receive email notifications at each stage of the review process.
