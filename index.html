Aims & Scope

The Out Of Ordinary journal is dedicated to publishing groundbreaking research that challenges the boundaries of conventional academia. Our peer review process involves a collective of beings from various dimensions of reality.

Scope Includes:

  • → Disruptive ideas that may advance human society but currently exist only as thoughts
  • → Hypotheses that cannot be proven wrong (in a good way)
  • → Highly subjective research
  • → Studies on thoughts that think about themselves

*"Break the boundaries of academia, record every great idea!"* — Editorial Board Manifesto, 2026

Publication Ethics:

All submissions undergo our proprietary "Double-Blind Consensual Reality Review" process. Reviewers may include the authors themselves, past selves, future selves, or parallel universe variants. Plagiarism is not tolerated, except from yourself in other timelines (credited accordingly).
