Call for Papers

Submission opportunities for extraordinary research

The Oddity of Everyday Life

We invite submissions exploring the extraordinary within the ordinary. From the peculiar patterns of daily routines to the hidden strangeness in mundane objects, this issue seeks to uncover the oddity that permeates our everyday existence. What secrets lie beneath the surface of normalcy? How can we reimagine the familiar through an extraordinary lens?

Submission Deadline

When the Stars Align (Approx. June 30, 2026)

Expected Publication

August 2026

Scope

We welcome submissions from all disciplines, including but not limited to: philosophy, sociology, psychology, art, literature, and speculative science.

Submission Guidelines

Eligibility

Submissions are open to researchers, artists, writers, and thinkers from all backgrounds. There are no formal qualifications required—only a passion for exploring the extraordinary.

Submission Process

Submit your manuscript through our online portal. All submissions will undergo a blind peer review process by our panel of extraordinary reviewers.

Accepted Formats

We accept manuscripts in .docx, .pdf, or .txt format. For multimedia submissions, please contact our editorial team directly.

Originality

All submissions must be original work. We do not accept previously published material or submissions under consideration by other journals.
